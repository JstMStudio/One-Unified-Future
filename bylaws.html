Bylaws

There are many rules for the One Unified Future alliance. All of these rules are made to keep this alliance safe and clean together. Please follow all of these rules to continue to be a member of this alliance. Failed to do so might result in a removal from the entire alliance.

These guidelines also applied to all company and team employees and members. If a company and team members or employees failed to follow these guidelines, action must be taken by the team or company or will result in a possible termination of membership from the alliance. One Unified Future currently uses athenian-like democracy structure to maintain the alliance. Every member's company and teams could vote on the alliance decisions etc. 1. Rule 1: Members. Each allied member's companies and teams could have a representative to the management legislative assembly. The maximum representative of each team and company would be 4. 1.1. Rule 1.1: Alliance community. However, alliance member companies and teams could let all of their members join the alliance community. 2. Rule 2: Clean Environment. Each allied member team and companies should have a safe and clean work environment. This alliance should have a safe and clean environment instead of a dangerous and toxic one. 2.1. Rule 2.1: Manner. All members are required to have manners while inside the alliance. Please do not use any bad words inside the alliance under any and all circumstances, even if you are mad and also not allowed. 2.2. Rule 2.2: Respectful. Each and all members should be respectful to one another at all times inside the alliance. We should and must pay respect to everyone no matter what their religion, nationality, color, gender etc. 2.3. Rule 2.3: Violence. Each and all members must not result in any sorts of violence inside the alliance. Violence of any kind like bullying, harassment, boycotting and fighting are not allowed and have no place in this alliance. 3. Rule 3: Languages. It is required that all communication inside the alliance be used in the alliance official languages. Other languages that aren’t the official languages in the alliance aren’t allowed. Please use English and Chinese only in the alliance. 4. Rule 4: Election. Each and every decision and roles in the alliance are required to be voted on in an election inside the alliance legislative assembly. All elections are required to be held before a decision is made. 4.1. Rules 4.1: Voting Results. It is required that all members agree to the voting results under any and all circumstances. Minority must agree with the majority too and after retaliation aren’t allowed. 4.2. Rule 4.2: Voting Circumstances. Some decisions might not be enforced to be voted on by the legislative assembly due to some circumstances. Circumstances that might result in: 4.2.1. Might get into legal issues. 4.2.2. Violate Malaysian laws. 4.2.3. Not technically possible. 4.2.4. Requirements are too high for the alliance. 4.2.5. Decision couldn’t or shouldn’t be voted on by the alliance legislative assembly. 5. Each and all members promoted to a role is required to be elected by the alliance legislative assembly. Every role is elected every half a year. 13.1. Rule 13.1: Lose. If you have lost an election, you are required to step down from your roles. You don’t allow yourself to choose not to step down from your position. 13.2. Rule 13.2: Unelectable role. Some roles are not electable by the alliance legislative assembly and members will automatically be promoted to the role. Unelectable role is permanent to the member unless they sign.
